# Blackjack
A realistic game of text-based blackjack, written entirely in python, and in one entire file (which I hear I'm not supposed to do, but I'm new here)
# Features
I included side bets, so you can gamble more

Pairs - If both of your cards are the same value, payout 3:1

21+3 - If your 2 cards plus the dealer's first are sequential, payout 10:1

3 of a Kind - If your 2 cards plus the dealer's first are all the same value, payout 30:1

Over/Under 13 - Is the combined value of your cards over or under 13? Nothing happens if it's 13, payout 2:1

Super 7s - If 1, 2, or 3 of the cards visible are 7s. Payout: 1 7 is 3:1, 2 7s is 50:1, all 3 is 100:1

# Possible Actions
The standard moves of blackjack apply:

Hit - get another card

Stand - stay with your current hand

Double Down - double your bet for one extra card, no moves permitted afterwards

Split - if both of your cards are the same, add another bet to have 2 separate hands, in which you can do all 3 actions stated above.
